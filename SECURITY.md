# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

We prioritize the security of the **CPS Enterprise Dynamics Commerce System (DCS)**. Professional-grade systems require consistent patching. We currently provide security support for the following versions:

| Version | Supported          | Notes                                         |
| :---    | :---:              | :---                                          |
| v2.x    | ✅ | Current stable release (Recommended)          |
| v1.8.x  | ✅ | Long-Term Support (LTS) through Dec 2026      |
| < v1.7  | ❌ | End of Life; please upgrade to a supported version |

---

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a potential security vulnerability, please **do not** open a public issue. In accordance with professional coordinated disclosure practices, follow these steps:

1.  **Contact:** Email your report to [security@example.com](mailto:security@example.com) (Replace with your official security alias).
2.  **Encryption:** For sensitive findings, please encrypt your message using our **PGP Public Key** (found in `SECURITY_PUB_KEY.asc` in the root directory).
3.  **Details:** To expedite the triage process, include:
    * A technical description of the vulnerability.
    * A Proof of Concept (PoC) or step-by-step instructions to reproduce.
    * The potential impact assessment (CVSS metrics if available).

---

## Our Response Process

Upon receipt of a valid report, the engineering team will:

* **Acknowledge:** Confirm receipt within **24–48 hours**.
* **Analyze:** Conduct a deep-dive architecture impact analysis.
* **Remediate:** Develop and test a security patch.
* **Disclose:** Issue a Security Advisory once the patch is ready for deployment.

---

## Disclosure Policy

We believe in responsible, coordinated disclosure. We ask that you:
* Give us a reasonable timeframe to remediate the issue before making any information public.
* Avoid privacy violations, destruction of data, or interruption of service during your research.
* Focus on the integrity of the system architecture.

---

## Recognition

While we do not currently operate a monetary bounty program, we value the expertise of the security community. Valid reports will be eligible for:
* Inclusion in our **Security Hall of Fame**.
* Public credit in the release notes (at your discretion).
* Professional recommendations for exceptional architectural insights.
